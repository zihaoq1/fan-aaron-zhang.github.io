Extension application

You can find useful information related to coursework extension at

https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/academic-advice/assessment-support/request-a-coursework-extension/

The deadline for the submission of an extension request is 48 hours before the coursework submission deadline. You need to work to your current due date until the school has approved an extension.


Exceptional circumstances

If the extension request deadline has passed or you have experienced something unexpected, unavoidable, and outside of your control, you can consider submitting an exceptional circumstance case. Detailed information can be found at

https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/academic-advice/assessment-support/exceptional-circumstances/
