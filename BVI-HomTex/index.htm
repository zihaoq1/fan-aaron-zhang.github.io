About

BVI-HomTex is a Homogeneous Video Texture Dataset. In the context of this dataset, homogeneity means that the textures are spatially and temporally consistent. It comprises 120 sequences with a spatial resolution of 256x256 pixels. The low resolution was chosen considering the size of the dataset to keep the encoding time manageable.
